---
title: 'Golden Age'
---

Golden Age is a period on SkatePark that lasts until TGE of $SKATE token.

All Ollies earned on Skate Shuffle during the Golden Age will be **2x**. 
The maximum Supply of Ollies will remain at **5 Billion**.

Any Visa multipliers will not apply during Golden Age.

To access Skate Shuffle, you will either need to own a Skate Visa or have a referral code. 
Referral codes can only be generated by Skate Visa holders. 
Skate Visa will no longer be sold after **February 21, 2025**.

During the Golden Age, a Jackpot will accumulate on each chain through Skate Shuffle.
